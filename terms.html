Check your FHIR®

A proof of concept app that checks if your FHIR web services are ready. Patient date retrieved will never be stored anywhere.

This is a Proof of Concept app that is to test your FHIR setups. This app does not store your health data on your device or on the server. This app does not share your health data to anyone else. The web browser could cache the data being retrieved and displayed, and you can save the data as a web page, but these are not features provided by the app.
